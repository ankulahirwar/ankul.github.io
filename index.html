About Prashasnik Gurukul

"Prashasnik Gurukul" - 'One Institution Complete Solution for civil services examinations (IAS/PCS)' was inaugurated on 13th of March,2020. We will cater to needs of civil services aspirants for preliminary, main as well as interview segments of civil services examinations. We will have separate batches for English and Hindi medium.

Special Features Of Test Series

SCP (Success Confirmation Programme) Printed Answers & be detailed explanantions.

2 Papers of CSAT (2 Comprehensive Paper/Mock Test) Nature & Pattern of Question will be as per UPSC Pattern.

Post Test, Question Paper discussion by faculties who are your mentors as well 10 Paper of General Studies.
